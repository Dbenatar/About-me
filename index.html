Bio

Hi, I am Dom. I am a professional chef wanting a career change into tech. This exercise and growth mindset as a whole makes me think about the first time I watched Jurassic Park (one of my favourite books and films) as a child. When found out that a lot of it was made using computers. Having no understanding of what that really meant, I just always put it down as the kind of thing I would never be able to learn. This year I have begun my journey into web development and while I have really struggled with some things, I have surprised and impressed myself when learning something new.

I am not a classically trained chef and have always learnt on the job. After many years I worked up to the level of "Sous chef" which is essentially the second in command under the head chef.

In a previous life before I was a full time chef, I worked in the speciality coffee industry. I was essentially a professional coffee snob. I was previously employed as the head barista and coffee roaster for Cambridge based Coffee shop, Hot Numbers Coffee. After years of tasting and drinking multiple coffees a day, sometime in the excess of 10. I now try limit that to 2 or 3 in the morning with a cut off of about 1-2 o'clock.

After a number of years of giving my all to the hospitalty industry I was left feeling burnt out and wanting more from a career. Ive always felt somewhat tech savvy and learning web development has been challenging but really rewarding to learn something new. At the start of this year I began a Full stack web development course with Codecademy.
